School of Data (SOD)

Designed for students who enjoy working with numbers and patterns, this school focuses on analyzing data to uncover actionable insights and building data-driven solutions.

Data Analysis: Learn techniques to clean, visualize, and interpret data using tools like Python, Power BI, and SQL.

Data Science: Explore advanced statistical and machine learning techniques to solve complex real-world problems.

Data Engineering: Build and maintain data infrastructures and pipelines to manage large-scale data processing.

School of Product

At SOP, students are equipped with the skills of creating and managing digital products that are user-centric and business-aligned. Students gain skills in product strategy, user experience, and team leadership.

Product Design: Develop user-friendly and aesthetic designs using tools like Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD.

Product Marketing: Learn strategies for promoting and positioning products effectively in competitive markets.

Product Management: Gain knowledge in project and product management to oversee teams and deliver successful digital solutions.

School of Engineering (SOE)

SOE is dedicated to helping you develop the technical expertise required to design, build, and secure digital systems. We prepare students for a wide range of roles in software development, system architecture, and security.

Frontend Engineering: Learn to create user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure visually appealing and user-friendly applications.

Backend Engineering: Master the development of server-side logic, databases, and APIs that power web and mobile applications.

Cloud Engineering: A deep-dive into cloud computing platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud to build scalable, secure, and efficient systems.

Cybersecurity: Master the art of protecting digital systems, networks, and sensitive data from cyber threats. Learn techniques in ethical hacking, encryption, and threat analysis to secure applications and infrastructures.
